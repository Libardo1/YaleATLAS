Welcome to the Yale ATLAS FEP (Frequently Encountered Problems) Page.

On this website, you will find a list of previously and frequently encountered problems along with their solution. The goal of this page is to learn from other graduate students' errors and quickly solve common issues. As the content grows, it will be organized in sections according to themes. This page is curated by @mickypaganini. Get in contact to obtain editor access or to point out typos or malfunctioning solutions.
