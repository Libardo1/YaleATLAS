Welcome to the Yale ATLAS F.E.P. Page

On this website, you will find a list of previously and frequently encountered problems along with their solution. The goal of this page is to learn from other graduate students' errors and quickly solve common issues. As the content grows, it will be organized in sections according to themes.

This page is curated by @mickypaganini. Get in contact to obtain editor access or to point out typos or malfunctioning solutions.

GRID

  1. Install GRID Certificate on lxplus

    Request a new certificate here: https://ca.cern.ch/ca/
    Once you have exported your certificate in .pem format, scp it to your ~/.globus/ folder and follow the simple instructions here: https://twiki.cern.ch/twiki/bin/view/Atlas/ObtainingGridCertificateAndVO#Prepare_the_certificate_to_run_o

  2. Monitoring Jobs

    You can monitor your grid submissions by going to http://bigpanda.cern.ch/ and entering your full name under "User".

  3. Rucio

    setupATLAS
    voms-proxy-init -voms atlas
    localSetupRucioClients
    rucio list-dids user.whatever_name:* % list all dids in that user's folder
    rucio list-dids user.whatever_name:*some_string* % list all dids in that user's folder that contain 'some_string' in their name
    rucio download user.whatever_name:user.whatever_name.whatever_fileorfolder % download stuff from the GRID
